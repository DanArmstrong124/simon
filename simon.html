Instructions

This is a game of Simon!

To play Simon, press start and click or tap the colours that light up after Simon has finished his turn.

(You will know he is finished once the colours stop flashing!)

You MUST click them in order, or you will be reset to Level 1...

Good luck... you win after Level 20!
